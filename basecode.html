Welcome to the Punt, Pass, Predict machine learning simulator


In a world fueled by rapid advancement in technology, we attempted to answer a single question...If you built a machine learning algorithm and trained it with over 10 years of historical NFL play-by-play data, could you accurately predict the next play in a live football game? The answer...yes!

Don't believe us? Try it out for yourself. Simply read the instructions below and follow-along during your favorite teams next game. Have fun!




How to play the game!


  1. Before the game begins, scroll down and preview the inputs. You will have to enter this information for every play fairly quickly, so it will help if you know what is required in advance.
  2. Before the game begins, select the home and away team for today's match-up and click submit.
  3. After the receiving team receives the initial kick-off enter the pre-play data and select submit. You must use proper formatting in the open text fields. If you do not use proper formatting the results will not be provided.
  4. Once you submit the pre-play data, the prediction for the next play will be displayed below.
  5. After the play is completed, enter the result for play type and yards in the two post-play fields and press submit.
  6. Repeat these steps for the entire game and see how many times the algorithm accurately predicts the next play.
